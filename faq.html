FAQ - Le Petit Paris

Welcome to the FAQ page of Le Petit Paris! Here, we’ve compiled answers to some of the most common questions we receive. If you have any other inquiries, feel free to contact us!

1. What are your opening hours?

We are open every day from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Come by for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

2. Do you take reservations?

Yes, we recommend making a reservation, especially during peak hours and weekends. You can reserve a table by calling us or using our online booking system.

3. What type of cuisine do you serve?

At Le Petit Paris, we offer a delightful selection of traditional French dishes, pastries, and coffee. Our menu includes options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, all prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

4. Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes to cater to different dietary preferences. Please check our menu or ask our staff for recommendations.

5. Is there parking available?

Yes, there is street parking available near the café. Additionally, we are located close to public transportation for your convenience.

6. Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Yes, we provide takeout for all our menu items. Delivery options are also available through popular delivery services. Check our website for more details.

7. Can I host an event at Le Petit Paris?

Yes! We offer event hosting for small gatherings and celebrations. Please contact us directly to discuss your needs and available options.

8. Are you pet-friendly?

We love pets! While we have a pet-friendly patio, we kindly ask that you check with our staff for specific seating arrangements.

9. Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes, we have a loyalty program for our valued customers. Join our program to earn points with every purchase, which can be redeemed for discounts and special offers.

10. How can I stay updated on events and promotions?

To stay updated, subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media. We frequently post about special events, menu changes, and exclusive offers.

11. Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering services for various events such as weddings, parties, and corporate gatherings. Please contact us for more details on our catering menu and pricing.

12. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. Please note that some payment methods may vary by location.

13. Are there gluten-free options available?

Yes, we have a selection of gluten-free dishes available. Please ask our staff for recommendations when you visit!

14. Can I purchase gift cards?

Absolutely! We offer gift cards that can be purchased in-store or online. They make great gifts for friends and family!

15. What should I do if I have food allergies?

If you have food allergies, please inform our staff when placing your order. We take food allergies very seriously and will do our best to accommodate your needs.

16. Do you have live music or events?

Yes, we host live music events and special themed nights. Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on upcoming events!

17. Is Wi-Fi available for customers?

Yes, we provide free Wi-Fi for our customers. You can enjoy browsing the internet while sipping on your favorite drink!

18. How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback! You can leave a review on our website or social media, or speak directly to our staff during your visit.

19. Do you offer any loyalty rewards or discounts?

Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards frequent customers with points for every purchase. You can redeem points for discounts and special offers.

20. Can I bring my own cake for a special occasion?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own cake for special occasions. Please inform our staff in advance, and we can help accommodate your needs!
